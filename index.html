About Our Organization

Ammaodi Organisation Home is a registered non-profit organization based in Hyderabad, Telangana. Our mission is to create a safe and nurturing environment for children in need, providing them with essential resources, education, and emotional support to help them thrive.

Founded with the vision of making a meaningful difference in the lives of underprivileged children, we work tirelessly to ensure every child has access to basic necessities, quality education, and opportunities for a brighter future.

Our organization operates from our facility in Medipally, Hyderabad, where we run various programs including after-school tutoring, nutritional support, healthcare assistance, and recreational activities for children from disadvantaged backgrounds.
